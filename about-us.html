About Us

Who we are

Cotton Box is an online store that was founded in March 2019 by Ema Ding.

What do we do?

We at Cotton Box sell premium designer Korean womens clothing for the Taiwanese market.

Each item is hand selected to ensure that the best quality and designs are selected, as we at Cotton Box believe in nothing but the best for our customers.
